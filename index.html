About

Local, hands-on, and proud of the result

Should've Been Us Landscaping is owned and run by Zeke Grabowski — a Black Mountain native who brings the same work ethic to every yard that he brings to the mat as a college wrestler.

A senior at Montreat College ('26), Zeke built this business on a simple idea: most yards could look a whole lot better with someone who actually cares about the details. Every job gets a premium finish — clean lines, healthy plantings, and a property you're proud to come home to.

When you hire Should've Been Us, you're working directly with the owner. No call centers, no rotating crews — just straight answers, fair estimates, and work that holds up.
